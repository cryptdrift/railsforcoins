How it works

1. Submit requirement form with relevant details about project and expectations

2. We will send you the proposal along with a message

3. Sign the message with your BTC/LTC/NMC address and send us the address and signature

4. We verify the message

5. Start working on your project

6. On completion of phase or task you release the funds

What is a Phase?

A phase is a part of a project. Big projects are divided into smaller phases for focus and sense of accomplishment. Usually projects are split into 10 to 15 hour phases. We have fixed price for each phase.

What is a task?

A task is a simple one of change that most likely takes a few hours. Tasks are charged hourly.

Lets be honest

Although we have these phase and hourly structures, we can't promise that our judgement about how long something will take. Software projects often miss deadlines but we will try our best to deliver in time.
